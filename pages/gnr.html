"Sweet Child O' Mine" - Guns N' Roses

"Sweet Child O' Mine" is a rock anthem by Guns N' Roses, featured on their debut album, *Appetite for Destruction* (1987). The track is renowned for its iconic guitar riff, powerful lyrics, and dynamic composition.

The song topped the Billboard charts and remains one of the greatest rock songs of all time, symbolizing the band's raw energy and creativity.
